Welcome!

Change and transformation are an inevitable and ongoing part of the human experience. All of us feel ill-equipped to meet life’s challenges at times. We may develop unhealthy coping skills, or cling to ways of thinking that do not serve us or make things worse. We can develop patterns of thinking and behaving in ways that leave us feeling overwhelmed, anxious, depressed, stuck, afraid, stagnant, traumatized, in pain, reactive, which can result in dysfunction and negatively affect every aspect of our lives and as well as our relationships with others.

Therapy is a process of discovery, growth and healing. Together, we find ways for you to move through and beyond life’s challenges. My role as a therapist is to actively listen and be fully present. Through deep, honest conversations and connection, I help people to discover what hurts, heal from past wounds, and find ways to reset, by building on strengths, co-developing positive coping skills that work, and finding strategies and ways of thinking in order to create more ease and contentment, to be more relaxed, present and happy, for a life that is more manageable and has purpose.

About

A licensed Master of Social Work, (LCSW) Susan works with individuals, couples and leads groups. She uses a blended approach to psycho-therapy, drawing from various evidence-based therapeutic practices including but not limited to Cognitive Behavior therapy (CBT), Dialectical behavior therapy (DBT), Psycho-education, Motivation interviewing (MI), Gestalt therapy, Mindfulness, Yoga philosophy, and the Strengths Perspective.
